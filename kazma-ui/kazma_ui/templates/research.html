🔍 Research Results

When you ask the agent to research a topic via the Swarm (using dispatch_swarm), the results are persisted here with full traceability:

  • Browse all research outputs — prompt, worker, cost, duration, full text.
  • Compare two runs side-by-side — see how cost, tokens, duration, and output text diverged.
  • Export any result to DOCX, PDF, or Markdown for academic or reporting use.

Research tasks are tagged with metadata.kind=research at dispatch time and stored in the Swarm TaskStore (kazma-data/swarm_tasks.db).
